Fambit is an open, decentralized, user-powered mechanism for appreciating content via Bitcoin microdonations

How it works

  1. User sends bitcoin to local Fambit wallet (picture of $ -> fambit)
  2. User visits websites (picture of user node looking at url or smth)
  3. Fambit donates percentage of remaining funds (picture of url, fambit attaching $)

Promotes decentralization

(picture of Reddit, LukeAtSea)
  • Donates to every site: so, even small popular-for-one-page sites will be funded
  • Author automatically receives bitcoin, no centralized collection (Brave[0])
  • Fambit doesn't take a "cut" of donations, unlike other solutions (Brave[1], SatoshiPay[2])

More friendly than ads, more flexible than crowdfunding

  • Unlike advertisements, absolutely no user tracking[3]
  • To crowdfund, a content creator usually must either be popular, or "go viral". Fambit allows making some revenue, even if fans don't immediately find the content
